Pasta Recipe

Description

A food made from flour, water, and sometimes egg, that is cooked and usually served with a sauce. It is made in various shapes that have different names: Spaghetti, lasagna, ravioli, and cannelloni are all types of pasta. Pasta is an italian originated dish but enjoyed all around the world. Several other dishes like "Mac n Cheese" were derived from pasta by other countries.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Preferred pasta type
  • Salt
  • Cheese (Mozarella is best suited)
  • Ingredients for preferred sauce type
  • Fresh Basil
  • Additional optional ingredients (Beef, chicken, etc)

How to make it:

  1. Make your preffered sauce(Bolognese, tomato, pesto, etc.)
  2. Get a pot of water on the stove./li>
  3. Boil the water.
  4. Add your preferred pasta type
  5. Cook halfway, add your pasta to a pan with your sauce and continue cooking.
  6. Let it rest for a minute or so.
  7. Enjoy!
